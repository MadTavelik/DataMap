Which New York County Leads in Solar Power?

The Answer Might Surprise You

12/15/2022 by Chris Janaro

One of many solar powered homes in Suffolk County, NY. Photo by Christopher Janaro

When one thinks of green energy and proponents of it, politically conservative Long Island, New York, isn't likely the first place that comes to mind. However, data provided by The New York State Energy Research and Development Authority point otherwise; at least when it comes to solar power. The data shows commercial and residential solar power projects in the state going back to the year 2000 and indicates a substantial lead in Suffolk County, NY, when compared to the rest of the state and some of its more liberal strongholds, such as NYC or Buffalo.
