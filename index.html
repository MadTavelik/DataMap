Seagirt Blvd in The Rockaways has had a high rate of accidents, with a total of 232 total injuries along the entire corridor between 2015 and 2019 and 12 severe pedestrian injuries. There is a lack of crosswalks or Bike lanes on the 8-lane roadway with rampant automobile speeding and jaywalking. In 2019, a camera at the intersection of Seagirt Boulevard and Crest Road issued the most speed camera tickets in the city; according to the Department of Transportation last year, it handed out 18,289 tickets, or roughly 50 per day. In 2017 after a jogger suffered a fatality on the Blvd, Assemblywoman. Stacey Pheffer Amato representing the Rockaways in New York’s 23rd district, called on the NYC DOT to immediately make improvements to the Blvd.
